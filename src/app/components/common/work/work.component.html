Ientify of the Users on Konnecteum is verified before they can work on Projects for building trust.

User Timeline on Blockchain

Blockchain Technology provides security and immutability to User Timeline which shows all activities of the User from Onboarding to the latest activity with complete accuracy and transparency.

Recommendation engine

Recommendation Engine recommends the right Gigpreneur to the right Project and the right Project to the right Gigpreneur.

Immutable reviews and Ratings

Reviews and ratings are stored on the immutable and tamperproof Blockchain. Good reviews stimulate the decision to buy and sell services, Increase confidence and build credibility.

Secure and trustworthy Payments

Gigpreneurs start work only after Employers deposit money in Nodal account and Employers pay only for the work they approve.

Live chat and call facility

Employers and Gigpreneurs can live chat or video call, share files and track progress of the Project.
