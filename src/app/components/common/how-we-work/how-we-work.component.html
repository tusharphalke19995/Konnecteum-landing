Post Project

  1. Tell us about your requirements.
  2. Specify the skills you need.
  3. Estimate your project budget.
  4. Decide your project duration.

Recommendations

  1. Get recommendations of the best matching talent from the pool of Gigpreneurs.
  2. Invite your favorite gigpreneurs if you wish.
  3. Hire the desired talent with ease.

Live Chat

  1. Connect with Project teams via live chats and video calls.
  2. Establish better connect with your teams.
  3. Share Files/ Screen while live chat and calls.

Review, Approve & Pay

  1. Review the workdone by gigpreneur.
  2. Approve or request rework till you are satisfied.
  3. Upon your approval, the payment will be directly sent to Gigpreneur's bank account already registered on Konnecteum.

Review & Ratings

  1. Enrich your Konnecteum profile by giving and receiving reviews and ratings.
  2. Improve your standing on the platform to attract good talent.
  3. Online reviews are the great way to find out the strength and skills of gigpreneur you want.
